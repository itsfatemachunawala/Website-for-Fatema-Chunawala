About me

Hi! I'm a 19-year-old graphic design student with an endless passion for all forms of art. From music to makeup, painting to movies, and designing to fashion, I find inspiration everywhere. Art, in all its forms, has been a constant in my life, and I love exploring how each medium tells its own unique story.

Painting is one of my favorite hobbies—it helps me unwind and express my creativity freely. I also use fashion as a way to express myself; dressing up feels like putting together a living, wearable piece of art.

Movies are another big source of inspiration for me, especially when I'm feeling unmotivated or stuck in a creative slump. Comedy movies are my go-to—they never fail to lift my mood and remind me to keep things lighthearted.

Being a Cancer, I'm deeply emotional and intuitive, which I think helps me connect with the world around me and translate that into my work. I also love food—whether it's a home-cooked meal or trying out a new cuisine, I'm always excited to explore new flavors.

Ultimately, I want to experience everything life has to offer. Whether it's traveling, trying new hobbies, or diving into creative challenges, I'm driven by a desire to live fully and create work that truly reflects my passion for life.
